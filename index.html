About

In an attempt to revive the magical Mumbai life, Bombae Square is focused on exploring the 4 corners of a square that best describes any city: the food, the people, the culture and the places. Held completely online, we will virtually take you to places where you made some of your best memories back when times were easier. It is an attempt to bring the refreshing Mumbai Life back to your home with fun events, workshops, podcasts, music, instagram lives, reels, posts, stories, zoom meetings, vlogs and much more.
